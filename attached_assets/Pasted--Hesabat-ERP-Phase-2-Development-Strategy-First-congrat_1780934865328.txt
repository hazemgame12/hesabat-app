# Hesabat ERP – Phase 2 Development Strategy

First, congratulations on completing Phase 1.

Before moving to additional modules, we need to align Hesabat with proven accounting ERP workflows instead of inventing new workflows from scratch.

## Development Direction

Use ERPNext and Odoo as functional references only.

Do NOT copy source code directly.

Instead, analyze their workflows, business logic, accounting relationships, document lifecycle, reports and posting behavior, then implement similar functionality inside Hesabat using our own codebase.

The objective is:

Build a simple, modern and easy-to-use accounting system for SMEs, not a complex enterprise ERP.

---

## Product Vision

Hesabat should focus on:

* Simplicity
* Fast data entry
* Excellent accounting reports
* Easy bank reconciliation
* Excel import/export
* Modern dashboard
* Clean user experience

Avoid unnecessary ERP complexity.

---

## Compare Current System Against ERPNext

Review the following modules and identify missing business logic:

### Accounting

* Chart of Accounts
* Journal Entries
* General Ledger
* Trial Balance
* Profit & Loss
* Balance Sheet

### Customers

* Customer Master
* Customer Statement
* Receipts Collection Cycle
* Accounts Receivable Workflow

### Suppliers

* Supplier Master
* Supplier Statement
* Payment Cycle
* Accounts Payable Workflow

### Inventory (Simple Version)

Inventory must remain simple:

Required:

* Item Master
* Units of Measure
* Goods Receipt
* Goods Issue
* Stock Balance
* Stock Movement Report

Not Required:

* Manufacturing
* BOM
* MRP
* Complex Warehouse Operations
* Advanced Inventory Planning

### Banking

Priority Module

Implement:

* Bank Accounts
* Cash Accounts
* Payment Vouchers
* Receipt Vouchers
* Bank Statement Excel Import
* Automatic Matching Engine
* Bank Reconciliation Report

This is one of the key competitive advantages of Hesabat.

---

## Deliverables Required

Before development starts:

1. Gap Analysis between Hesabat and ERPNext workflows.
2. Missing business logic list.
3. Database changes required.
4. APIs required.
5. Screen changes required.
6. Development roadmap by module.

---

## Important Rule

Do not create new screens unless they serve a real accounting workflow.

Priority is accounting functionality, usability and automation, not increasing the number of pages.

The goal is to make Hesabat feel easier than ERPNext and Odoo while keeping the most important accounting capabilities.
